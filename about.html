About University CGPA Calculator

Your all-in-one platform to calculate CGPA, access study tips, and find academic resources for Nigerian universities.

Our Mission

We aim to empower Nigerian university students with easy-to-use tools and resources to track and improve their academic performance. Our CGPA calculator follows the Nigerian 5.0 grading scale, ensuring accurate results for every student.

What We Offer

  • A professional 5.0 scale CGPA calculator tailored for Nigerian universities.
  • Practical study tips and strategies to improve grades.
  • Direct links to academic research websites and journals.
  • Consistent updates with new resources and guidance for students.

Why Choose Us?

Our platform is designed specifically for Nigerian students. We combine accurate calculations, reliable resources, and a professional interface to make academic success easier and more accessible.
